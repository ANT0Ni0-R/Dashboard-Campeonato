## Multi-panel layouts

Default is one full-width chart; multi-panel is for genuine two-view stories, not to fill space.

- **Horizontal split** — two charts side by side (~45% each), e.g. a waterfall + a share breakdown. A single action title spans both.
- **Vertical split (shared X)** — line metric on top (~25% height), bars below (hero, ~50%); independent Y axes, X ticks only on the bottom panel. Use for e.g. a share-line over a stacked volume.

KPI cards and multi-panel executive summaries are exceptions — only if they add information a single chart cannot.
